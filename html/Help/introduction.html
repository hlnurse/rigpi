RigPi Introduction

 

MFJ-1234D

RigPi™ Station Server v 4.0

 

 

RigPi is a product developed by MFJ Enterprises, a company specializing in amateur radio equipment. RigPi is a remote control system designed to allow remote operation and control of amateur radio stations. It consists of hardware and software components that enable remote access to your radio equipment from a computer, tablet, or smart phone.

The core of RigPi is a Raspberry Pi computer, which acts as a central control unit. It connects to your radio equipment and provides a web-based interface for remote control and monitoring. The software includes features such as virtual control panels, audio streaming, logging capabilities, and integration with popular logging and digital mode software.

RigPi allows amateur radio operators to operate their stations from a remote location, enabling them to access and control their equipment over the internet. This can be especially useful for operators who want to operate their stations while away from home or for those who wish to share their equipment with others.
